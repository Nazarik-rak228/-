#### 1. General questions (Yes/No)

1. Is social media used differently in various countries around the world?
2. Has technology made the world smaller?

#### 2. Special questions (Wh-questions)

1. What percentage of teenagers are estimated to be victims of Internet crime?
2. Where do many cyber attacks seem to originate according to the recent survey?

#### 3. Alternative questions

1. Do people in Japan and the US use social media mainly to stay in touch with friends or to research products to buy online?
2. Are cyber criminals usually teenagers themselves or adults hiding behind their computers?

#### 4. Tag-questions (разделительные)

1. Social media helps us keep up with news and events worldwide, doesn't it?
2. It is difficult to catch cyber criminals because they hide behind their computers, isn't it?

#### 5. Subject questions (вопросы к подлежащему)

1. Who hacked 1.3 million computers a couple of years ago?
2. What has made it easier to commit crime?
