What is a computer? 
_Generally speaking,_ a computer is an electronic machine that processes data using hardware and software. _In simple terms,_ it allows us to perform tasks like browsing, working, or gaming by executing programmed instructions.

**3. What are the components of a computer?**  
_Basically,_ a computer consists of hardware like the processor, memory, storage, and peripherals such as a keyboard. _Of course,_ it also needs software and firmware to actually function.

**5. Have you ever had a virus on your computer? What did you do to fix it?**  
Unfortunately, I caught a virus on my old computer; it filled up all my RAM. I had to reinstall Windows because I couldn't control the computer. For many other viruses, using antivirus software is enough. Here's a common virus prevention guide:

**1. Install antivirus software**  
First of all, you should install reliable antivirus software.  

**2. Run regular scans**  
 it is important to run regular scans of your system.  

**3. Update your software**  
 Always keep your operating system and programs updated.  

**4. Be careful with emails**  
_Never open suspicious attachments or links in emails.  

**5. Avoid untrusted websites**  
Try to avoid downloading files from untrusted websites.  

**6. Use a firewall**  
A firewall helps block unauthorized access to your computer.  

**7. Backup your data**  
Regularly backup your important data to an external drive or cloud.  


**8. Use safe mode for removal**  
If you have a virus, restart your computer in safe mode to remove it.  


**9. Use virus removal tools**  
_Special removal tools can help clean infected files.  


**10. Reinstall the system if necessary**  
_Example:_ In extreme cases, you may need to completely reinstall the operating system.
